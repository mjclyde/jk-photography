Hi! I'm Jamie, a natural light photographer based in beautiful Cache Valley Utah. I picked up a camera in 2010 and haven't been able to put it down. I am forever inspired by families and passionate about documenting them. Think you've got a rambunctious crew? BRING IT ON! As the oldest of 7 children, and a mother to 5 of my own, I am no stranger to chaos!
Every family has their own unique vibe and connection - an exclusive one-of-a-kind love. Capturing this energy is my favorite part of the job!
I am a seeker of the light, in all its forms.
I have always been fascinated by the light. The way it dances across the water, elongates the shadows, and shimmers through the trees. It literally amplifies and gives life to everything it touches. My favorite kind of light is the kind that shines from the inside out. Light with a capital “L” that illuminates the heart and sparkles in the eye of all living creatures. I have a knack for noticing this kind of light… and one of the greatest joys in my life is photographing it.

One of my favorite phrases is

"I've got you."

I say it to my friends, I say it to my kids. I even say it to myself! (Gotta have your own back, right?!) And today I’m saying it to you.

I know how important your family pictures are to you. How do I know? Because you’re here.

By the time a family arrives at their session they have already invested time, money, and a heck of a lot of mental energy. Between aligning everyone's schedule and figuring out what on earth everyone is going to wear, it can feel pretty stressful!

But don’t you worry about a thing my friend. I’ve got you. Your only job is to show up and love on your people the best way you know how. I’ll take care of the rest.

We’ll strike a balance between posing you so you look amazing (pinky swear!) and letting things unfold naturally so we capture the real you! And the best news of all? You don’t have to worry about managing your kids at all during our session. You can totally leave that up to me (I’ve got a few tricks up my sleeves!)

Fun is always the goal, because I want your kids to be excited when you invite me back!
